Personal Introduction

My name is Masahide Yanagisawa, and I am a UI/UX Designer who enjoys creating simple and user-friendly designs. I like making interfaces that are clean, easy to use, and visually clear. My goal is to design experiences that users can understand and enjoy.

I focus on designing with the user in mind by improving usability and layout. I enjoy creating wireframes and simple prototypes, and I am always learning to improve my design skills.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games. Games help me understand how users interact with interfaces and how good design improves the experience. This hobby inspires me to create better and more engaging designs.
